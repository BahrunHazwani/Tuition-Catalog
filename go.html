BESTARI SKOR TUITION CENTER






COMPANY PROFILE

Bestari SKOR A was established in 1991 by a group of Educationists and Expert Teachers. Thanks for your supports that enable us to continue rendering the Best Education in Penang.

Bestari SKOR A has helped more than 20 Thousands of students in pursuing their Dreams over the years. They have achieved great milestone in Academics and Careers.

Every year, Hundreds of UPSR and PT3 students enroll in Prestigious Boarding school such as MRSM, Institute Yayasan Saad @ Melaka, Malay College Kuala Kangsar etc. More than 80% of SPM students enrolled in Medicine, Pharmacy, Law, Architects, Actuary, Engineering, Optometric, Dentistry etc. They further their tertiary education in overseas such as Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, India, England, Russia, USA etc. Success is made possible by Commitment, Perseverance and Hard-Work.
